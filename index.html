When

Saturday, May 31, 2014
5:30 pm

Where

Our celebration will take place at Lighthouse Point Park in New Haven Connecticut. The address is 2 Lighthouse Rd, New Haven, CT 06512. There is plenty of parking and it is free.

The ceremony will be on the beach at the South West part of the park, followed by a reception dinner and dance party at the Carousel Pavilion location right next to the ceremony site, just a short walk away.

How

For our out of town guests, Bradley International Airport is the most convenient
(airport code BDL).


Hyatt Place Milford is offering us special rates for our wedding weekend.
Reservations must be made by May 3rd for the deal to stand!!

1) To get the rate click the link above
2) Enter Check in date: 5/30/14 or 5/31/14
3) Enter Check out date: 5/31/14 or 6/1/14
4) Click on Special Rates Tab under Check Availability
5) Enter g-HC14 into the Corporate/Group# box.
6) Click on Check Availability Button and our rate (Hibler) should appear ($115 per room per night).

The Hotel is a 20 minute drive from lighthouse point park. Transportation will be provided to the reception at 5:00pm and back at the end of the night.

New Haven spring weather is typically mild (60s - 70s), but could get chilly once the sun goes down. The ceremony will be outside and the reception will be inside, but be sure to bring a sweater or light jacket just in case.

If you have any questions about any of the above, feel free to email us at HiblerCarlos@gmail.com.
